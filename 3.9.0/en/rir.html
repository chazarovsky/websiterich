RICH IT ID Recognition “RIR”

Take advantage of the optical character recognition used to obtain digitized credential information (IFE, INE and passport).

Do you want to easily obtain information such as text and images from a digitized credential? Rich it helps you to do it through the use of ROC (Optical Character Recognition) technology which will deliver all the values of each type of IFE and INE credential of types A, B, C, D and E, and passports, in a matter of seconds.


Rich it data scientists have specialized training, experience and tools that have allowed to develop RIR and obtain values and information by fields. The fields found from image to text on the front of the credential are: name, first surname, second surname, street, colony, zip code, state, population, CURP, voter code, registration year, section, location, issuance and validity. On the back you get the credential folio and the CIC.
