Rich IT Data Maturity Model “RDM²”

Analysis and development of Data Governance Maturity

RDM² will help you identify the current situation in regards to data management within your company and will lay the groundworks for the desired state in order to support, expand and potentiate your Analytics & Business Intelligence efforts with high quality & available near real time data. This way you will have efficient and reliable management for your most important assets.


BENEFITS

  • Identify key activities oriented at processes, people, technologies and data in order to achieve an efficient and auditable data management practice.
  • Promote comprehension, development and adoption of the best data management practices available in order to have trustworthy data.
  • Generate high value Business Intelligence with with reliable, high quality data & identify new business strategies to monetize those insights.

¿WHAT’S INCLUDED?

  • Business comprehension sessions with IT & management.
  • Analysis and documentation of the current state of data management.
  • Mapping of current and desired states of data maturity within the organization based on CMMI-DMM & DAMA* methodologies.
  • Development of a continuous improvement plan with key activities in order to achieve the desired data maturity state.*
  • Recommendations on future commercial strategies focused on data exploitation.

-Deliverables are denoted by *

Duration: 20 Workdays.
